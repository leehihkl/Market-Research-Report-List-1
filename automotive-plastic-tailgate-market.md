<p><strong>The Automotive Plastic Tailgate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Tailgate</li><li>Thermoset Tailgate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive plastic tailgate market is categorized into two main types: thermoplastic and thermoset tailgates. Thermoplastic tailgates are made from plastics that can be reheated and reshaped, offering flexibility, lightweight properties, and ease of recycling. They are often favored for their impact resistance and design versatility. In contrast, thermoset tailgates are created through a curing process, resulting in a rigid structure that is heat-resistant and durable but cannot be remolded. Both types cater to specific requirements in vehicle manufacturing.</p></p>
