<p><p>The current sensor market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Hall Effect Current Sensors** use magnetic fields to measure current, offering isolation and high accuracy.</p><p>2. **Shunt Based Current Sensors** measure voltage drop across a shunt resistor, providing direct current measurements at low cost.</p><p>3. **xMR Current Sensors** use magnetoresistive technology, combining features of Hall Effect and other sensors for improved sensitivity.</p><p>4. **Others** include various specialized sensors like Rogowski coils and optical sensors, catering to niche applications in diverse industries.</p></p>
