<p><strong>The High Purity Isobutylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MTBE Decomposition Method</li><li>C4 Fraction Separation Method</li><li>Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Purity Isobutylene market primarily encompasses three production methods. </p><p>The MTBE Decomposition Method involves breaking down methyl tert-butyl ether, a gasoline additive, producing isobutylene as a byproduct. </p><p>The C4 Fraction Separation Method separates isobutylene from other butanes and butylenes through distillation processes. </p><p>The Isobutane Dehydrogenation Method converts isobutane into isobutylene by removing hydrogen in high-temperature reactors. Each of these methods contributes to the purity and quality required for applications in chemical production and fuel additives.</p></p>
