<p><strong>The Air Freight Forwarding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Charter</li><li>Split Charter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air freight forwarding market comprises two main types: full charter and split charter. A full charter involves renting an entire aircraft for transporting goods, offering dedicated capacity and flexibility for large shipments. In contrast, a split charter shares the aircraft space among multiple shippers, optimizing costs for smaller loads while still providing faster transportation compared to traditional methods. Both options cater to different freight needs, balancing between cost-efficiency and capacity requirements in global logistics.</p></p>
