<p><p>The commercial ceiling fan market is primarily divided into AC ceiling fans and DC ceiling fans. AC ceiling fans use alternating current motors and are known for their robust performance and widespread availability, making them a cost-effective choice for many businesses. In contrast, DC ceiling fans utilize direct current motors, offering greater energy efficiency and quieter operation, which can reduce electricity costs over time. Both types serve commercial spaces, enhancing comfort and air circulation in various environments.</p></p>
