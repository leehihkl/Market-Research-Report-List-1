<p><p>The ultrasonic carving machine market is segmented into three primary types: CNC, manually-controlled, and PLC-controlled machines. CNC machines utilize computer numerical control for precision and automation, allowing for complex designs with minimal manual intervention. Manually-controlled machines require operators to guide the carving process, providing more hands-on control but less efficiency. PLC-controlled machines use a programmable logic controller to automate tasks while allowing for flexibility in operation, striking a balance between automation and manual oversight in various applications.</p></p>
