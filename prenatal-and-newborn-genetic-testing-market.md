<p><strong>The Prenatal and Newborn Genetic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Array-Comparative Genetic Hybridization(aCGH)</li><li>Fluorescence In-Situ Hybridization(FISH)</li><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction(PCR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prenatal and newborn genetic testing market comprises several advanced technologies. Array-Comparative Genetic Hybridization (aCGH) allows for the detection of chromosomal imbalances by comparing a test sample to a reference genome. Fluorescence In-Situ Hybridization (FISH) enables visualization of specific DNA sequences on chromosomes, providing insights into genetic abnormalities. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies small DNA segments for analysis, facilitating the identification of genetic mutations. Each method offers unique advantages in diagnosing and managing genetic conditions early in development.</p></p>
