<p><p>The Regenerative Thermal Oxidizer (RTO) market can be categorized into two main types: rotary and compact systems. Rotary RTOs utilize a rotating bed of ceramic media to enhance heat recovery, optimizing energy efficiency for larger industrial applications. Compact RTOs, on the other hand, are designed for smaller spaces, combining efficiency with a reduced footprint, making them suitable for facilities with limited space. Both types effectively eliminate volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other pollutants, contributing to environmental compliance.</p></p>
