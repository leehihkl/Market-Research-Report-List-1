<p><strong>The Flotation Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell-to-Cell Flotation Cell</li><li>Free-Flow Flotation Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flotation cell market comprises primarily two types: cell-to-cell flotation cells and free-flow flotation cells. Cell-to-cell flotation cells feature individual compartments that promote efficient separation of minerals by enhancing bubble-particle contact. This design supports better control of the flotation process. In contrast, free-flow flotation cells allow for a continuous flow of slurry through the tank, offering higher throughput and lower operational costs. Both types cater to varying processing needs in mining and mineral extraction industries.</p></p>
