<p><p>The underwater remotely operated vehicles (ROVs) market is categorized into three primary types: Micro ROVs, Lightwork-class ROVs, and Heavywork-class ROVs. Lightwork-class ROVs are designed for moderate-duty applications, handling tasks like underwater welding and repairs. Heavywork-class ROVs are robust systems built for demanding operations in deep waters, such as offshore construction and resource extraction, equipped with powerful tools and advanced manipulative capabilities.</p></p>
