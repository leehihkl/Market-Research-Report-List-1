<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Medical Cordless Microdrill</li><li>Electric Medical Cordless Microdrill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Cordless Microdrill market comprises two main types: pneumatic and electric microdrills. Pneumatic Medical Cordless Microdrills operate using compressed air, providing lightweight and highly maneuverable options for surgical procedures, often favored for their reliability and precision. In contrast, Electric Medical Cordless Microdrills utilize rechargeable battery systems, offering greater torque and consistent power without the need for an air supply, which enhances portability and ease of use during complex surgeries. Both types are essential for modern surgical applications.</p></p>
