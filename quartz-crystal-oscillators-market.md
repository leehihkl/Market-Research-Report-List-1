<p><p>Quartz Crystal Oscillators are categorized into several types based on their frequency stability and temperature characteristics. SPXO (Standard Parallel Crystal Oscillators) offer basic performance without temperature compensation. TCXO (Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators) provide enhanced stability across temperature variations. VCXO (Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillators) allow frequency adjustment via an external voltage. OCXO (Oven Controlled Crystal Oscillators) maintain precise frequency stability by heating the crystal to a controlled temperature. The overall quartz crystal market caters to diverse applications, including telecommunications and consumer electronics.</p></p>
