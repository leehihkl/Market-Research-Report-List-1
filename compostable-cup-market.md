<p><p>The compostable cup market features two main types: paper-coated products and thermoforming products. Paper-coated cups are made from paper materials coated with biodegradable polymers, offering a sustainable option for serving beverages while ensuring functionality and insulation. Thermoforming cups, on the other hand, are created through a molding process that shapes biodegradable plastics into specific designs, providing versatility and durability. Both types serve eco-conscious consumers seeking environmentally friendly disposable options, aligning with the growing demand for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
