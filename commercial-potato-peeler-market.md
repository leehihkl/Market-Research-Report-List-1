<p><strong>The Commercial Potato Peeler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Commercial Abrasive Potato Peeler</li><li>Commercial Steam Potato Peeler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial potato peeler market features two main types: abrasive and steam peelers. Commercial abrasive potato peelers use friction to remove potato skins through rotating brushes or abrasive surfaces, making them efficient for high-volume operations. In contrast, commercial steam potato peelers utilize steam to heat the potatoes, loosening the skin for easy peeling. This method is often preferred for its gentleness on the product and minimal waste, making it ideal for processing facilities focused on quality and efficiency.</p></p>
