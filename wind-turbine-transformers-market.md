<p><strong>The Wind Turbine Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-filled Transformer</li><li>Dr-type Transformer</li><li>Combined-type Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind turbine transformers market comprises three main types: oil-filled transformers, dry-type transformers, and combined-type transformers. Oil-filled transformers utilize oil for insulation and cooling, offering high efficiency and durability in outdoor settings. Dry-type transformers, on the other hand, use air for cooling and are preferred for their safety and environmental benefits, making them suitable for indoor installations. Combined-type transformers integrate features of both oil-filled and dry-type, providing versatility for various applications in wind energy systems.</p></p>
