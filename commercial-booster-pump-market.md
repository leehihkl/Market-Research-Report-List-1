<p><strong>The Commercial Booster Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stages</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial booster pump market is categorized into single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps feature one impeller and are typically used for low to moderate pressure applications, making them efficient and simpler to maintain. Multi-stage pumps, on the other hand, consist of multiple impellers stacked in series, enabling them to achieve higher pressures suitable for demanding irrigation, fire protection, and water supply systems. Both types cater to various commercial needs based on pressure requirements and system design.</p></p>
